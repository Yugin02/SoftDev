Renting a Motorcycle

What are the requirements to rent a motorcycle?

  • - To rent a motorcycle, you must be at least 18 years old, posses a valid motorcyle license, and provide a valid ID.

    • Do you provide a rental agreement or contact?

  • - Yes, we provide a rental agreement outlining the terms and conditions of your rental. You'll review and sign this agreement during the booking process.

    • Is there a minimum rental duration?

  • - Our rentals typically have a minimum duration of one day, but you can rent for longer periods.

    • Do you offer any rental packages or discounts for longer-term rentals?

  • - Yes, we often provide discounts for longer-term rentals. Check our website for details on available packages.

    • What is included in the rental price

  • - The rental price usually includes the motorcyle and basic insurance coverage. Additional services and gear, like helmets and gas, may be available for an extra fee.

    • Returning a Motorcycle

    What is the process for returning a rental motorcycle?

  • Returning a rental motorcycle is straightforward. Simply bring it back to the rental location, complete the necessary paper work, and our team will inspect the motorcyle.

    • Can I return the motorcycle to a different location from where I pick it up?

  • Generally, motorycle should be returned to the same location where you picked them up. Contact us for any special return arrangements.

    • What happens if I return the motorcycle late?

  • If you return the motorcycle late, additional fees may apply.

    • Is there a grace period for returning the motorcycle?

  • We typically offer a 30-minutes grace period for returning the motorycle without incurring late fees.
